Popular Blood Tests

Discover your health status with our popular Basic Health Panel.

Complete Blood Count (CBC)

This test analyzes blood components to assess health and diagnose conditions by examining levels of various substances.

Fasting Blood Sugar (FBS)

Discover the essential screening test for diabetes, measuring glucose after fasting, offering invaluable insights into your health.

Covid-19 Test

The COVID-19 test, a vital tool for detecting the virus, provides peace of mind and ensures safety for yourself and others.

Thyroid Function Test (TFTs)

This Test is crucial for assessing thyroid health and hormone levels, offering vital insights into overall well-being.

Liver Function Test (LFT)

This is critical in evaluating liver health, detecting abnormalities, and safeguarding overall well-being.

Hemoglobin A1c (HbA1c)

Hemoglobin A1c (HbA1c) provides valuable insights into long-term blood sugar levels, crucial for managing diabetes.
